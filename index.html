AP Microeconomics

Study the principles of economics that apply to the behavior of individuals within an economic system. You'll use graphs, charts, and data to analyze, describe, and explain economic concepts.

When we meet

We have weekly sessions on AP Microeconomics second semester

AP Macroeconomics

You'll explore how changes in aggregate spending and production, economic fluctuations, and policy actions affect national income, unemployment, and inflation. Topics may include: Aggregate demand. Short-run and long-run aggregate supply. Equilibrium and changes in the Aggregate Demand-Aggregate Supply Model.

When We Meet

We have AP Macroeconomic sessions first semester

Competitions

We will have 6+ Competitions this year! Some of them include the National Economics Challenge, Econ Bowl, and the Harvard Econ Essay contest. Keep an eye out for our meetings to learn more.
